## ?? How to Use This Board

### Weekly Workflow

**Monday Morning:**
- Review what was completed last week
- Move completed cards to ? COMPLETED
- Pick 3-5 cards to work on this week
- Assign owners (if team) or set priorities (if solo)

**During the Week:**
- Work through cards in order (Phase 1 ? 2 ? 3 ? 4)
- Add checklists within cards for subtasks
- Comment with updates and decisions
- Move blocked cards to ?? BLOCKED

**Friday Afternoon:**
- Review progress
- Update card statuses
- Plan next week's priorities
